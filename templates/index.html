The Challenge

Forest fires in Algeria pose significant environmental, economic, and social challenges. They devastate natural ecosystems, threaten wildlife habitats, and contribute to air pollution, exacerbating climate change. Additionally, these fires pose severe risks to human life and property, straining firefighting resources and emergency response systems.

Our Solution

Using machine learning and environmental data, we predict the Fire Weather Index (FWI) to aid in prevention and resource allocation.
